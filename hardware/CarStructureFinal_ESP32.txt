Components :-
4 motors
1 ESP 32
1 UltraSonic Sensor
1 Breadboard
1 L289N
1 Battery Source

Motor Driver - L289N Connections :-

Output 1 -> +ve Right Motors
Output 2 -> -ve Right Motors
Output 3 -> +ve Left Motors
Output 4 -> -ve Left Motors

+ 12 V -> connected to +ve rail , (then connected to battery)
GND    -> connected to -ve rail , (then connected to battery)
+5 V   -> connected to +ve rail , (different rail then +12 V )

Enable A -> D33
Enable B -> D32

Input 1 -> D14
Input 2 -> D27
Input 3 -> D26
Input 4 -> D25


Battery Connections :-
+ve -> to +ve rail(where motor driver's 12 V is connected)
-ve -> to all gnds and -ve's

Ultra Sonic SEnsor Connections 
VCC -> 3V3
Trig -> D4
Echo -> D5
GND -> GND
