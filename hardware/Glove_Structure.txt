1 Arduino Nano
1 NRF24L01 + Adapter
1 Breadboard Mini
1 Battery Source (9V)
1 MPU6050(Accelerator & Gyroscope)

VCC  -> +5V
GND  -> GND Nano
CE   -> D8
CS   -> D9
CLK  -> D13

GND  -> GND Nano

SDA  -> D18/A4
XDA  ->
XCL  ->
ADO  ->
INT  ->
